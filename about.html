Our Story

Founded in 2010, SoleHaven began as a small local shoe store in Nairobi with a big dream: to provide quality footwear that combines style, comfort, and affordability. Today, we're proud to be one of Kenya's leading shoe retailers, both in-store and online.

Our Mission

At SoleHaven, we're committed to helping you put your best foot forward. We believe that great shoes are the foundation of great style and comfort. Our mission is to offer a diverse range of high-quality footwear that caters to all walks of life.
